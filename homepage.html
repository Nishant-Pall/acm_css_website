Punjab Engineering College ACM Student Chapter introduces its members to a whole new learning culture and an environment where everyone can prosper from learning with each other and learning from each other. We also provide a platform to showcase your various talents in many different and innovative contests and competitions that are organized within and outside Punjab Engineering College.



Orientation 2k19

Interesting activities like Bread Jam Activity inspired by CS50, Human Sorting, were organized for the new birds of our College to give a taste of Computer Science.

Local Hack Day

The first event conducted by PEC ACM student chapter in 2019-20 which witnessed an audience of 250. Workshops on AWS, Data Science along with an hour long Code Battle were organized.

5 Technical Events namely Cyber Security Hackathon, Ultimate Coding Showdown, Code Relay, Andhadhun Coding and a session by famous YouTuber Gaurav Sen.
